Privacy Policy for Sortiago.

This privacy policy applies to Sortiago, a relaxing color sort puzzle game by Wallaroo Studio.

Effective date: July 10, 2026

App name: Sortiago

Package reference: com.wallaroo.sortiago

Responsible party: Wallaroo GmbH, Bonhoefferstr. 31, 90473 Nürnberg, Germany

Contact: support@wallaroo.studio

Overview

Sortiago is a relaxing color sort puzzle game. Wallaroo Studio aims to keep the app experience simple and privacy-conscious. This policy explains what data may be processed when you use Sortiago and which third-party services may be involved.

Current App Status

  • Sortiago uses Google Play Billing for optional one-time products.
  • Sortiago uses Google User Messaging Platform for consent collection where required.
  • The current release does not serve ads yet.
  • Future versions may use Google AdMob rewarded or interstitial ads after consent where required.
  • Sortiago does not require account sign-in.
  • Sortiago does not include user-generated content.
  • Sortiago does not include social, chat, or messaging features.
  • Sortiago does not share precise location.
  • Sortiago does not provide health, financial, or government features.

Optional Purchases

Sortiago may offer optional one-time products through Google Play Billing. Purchases are processed by Google Play, and Wallaroo Studio does not receive your full payment card details.

Optional products may include:

  • Remove Ads
  • Starter Pack
  • Premium Bundle

Purchase-related data may include purchase status, product identifiers, transaction confirmations, and other information provided by Google Play Billing that is necessary to deliver and verify purchases.

Consent Flow

Sortiago uses Google User Messaging Platform to request and manage consent where legally required. Consent choices may affect whether personalized advertising or certain advertising technologies can be used in future versions.

Advertising

The current release of Sortiago does not serve ads. Future versions may use Google AdMob rewarded ads or interstitial ads after consent where required. If ads are enabled in a future release, Google and its partners may process data such as device identifiers, approximate location, ad interactions, consent status, and technical information to provide, measure, and improve ads.

Accounts and User Content

Sortiago does not require account sign-in and does not provide user-generated content, public profiles, social posting, chat, or messaging features.

Location and Sensitive Categories

Sortiago does not request precise location sharing and is not designed for health, financial, or government services. The app is a casual puzzle game.

Technical Data and Diagnostics

Depending on your device, operating system, Google Play settings, and future app configuration, technical information such as device type, app version, operating system version, consent status, purchase status, and crash or diagnostic information may be processed to operate the app, support purchases, comply with platform requirements, and improve reliability.

Third-Party Services

Sortiago may use the following Google services:

  • Google Play Billing for optional one-time products.
  • Google User Messaging Platform for consent collection and consent state management.
  • Google AdMob in future versions for rewarded or interstitial ads after consent where required.

These services are provided by Google and may process data according to Google's own terms and privacy policies.

Children

Sortiago is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent where required by law. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact support@wallaroo.studio.

Data Retention

Wallaroo Studio retains support communications and purchase-support records only as long as reasonably necessary for support, legal, accounting, security, and operational purposes. Data processed by Google services is retained according to Google's policies and user settings.

Your Choices and Rights

You can manage purchases through Google Play, manage app permissions through your device settings, and update consent choices where the consent message is available. Depending on applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain data processing.

Contact

For privacy questions, purchase support, or Sortiago support, contact Wallaroo Studio at support@wallaroo.studio.

Changes to This Policy

Wallaroo Studio may update this privacy policy when Sortiago changes, when third-party services change, or when legal or platform requirements change. The effective date above indicates the latest version.
